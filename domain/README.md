## Core Concepts

### Options Flow
Real-time stream of options trades across US exchanges. Large or unusual trades may signal institutional activity or upcoming price moves.

### Stocks
Equity securities representing ownership in a company. Identified by ticker symbols (e.g., AAPL, TSLA).

### Options
Derivative contracts giving the right to buy (call) or sell (put) an underlying stock at a specified price (strike) before expiration.

### Strategies
Combinations of options and/or stock positions designed to achieve specific risk/reward profiles (e.g., iron condor, bull call spread).

### Watchlists (Playlists)
User-created collections of tickers for monitoring and analysis. Limited by subscription tier.

### Focus Mode
Keyboard-first navigation mode for power users to quickly analyze symbols.

## Data Sources

- **Real-time options flow** - Live trades from US exchanges
- **Stock quotes** - Current prices, volume, market cap
- **Options chains** - All available contracts by expiration/strike
- **Greeks** - Delta, gamma, theta, vega calculations
- **Earnings calendar** - Upcoming earnings dates
- **News** - Market news by symbol
